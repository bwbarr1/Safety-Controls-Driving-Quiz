Welcome to the Safety Control Quiz

This game will help you learn to identify different types of safety controls used in vehicles and workplaces.

Types of Controls

Direct Control: Actions you take yourself (e.g., putting on a seatbelt).

Engineering Control: Built-in safety features (e.g., airbags, lane assist).

Administrative Control: Policies or training (e.g., safety signage).

Better Than Nothing: Not ideal, but still helpful (e.g., rear-view mirror).

How to Play

You'll see a scenario and choose all applicable control types. Some questions have more than one correct answer.
